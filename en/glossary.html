Glossary

Characters

Shinji Ikari: The series' main protagonist, Shinji is a young Eva pilot, and his emotional struggles are central to the story.

Rei Ayanami: A key character in the story, Rei is a clone with deep ties to the Evas and the overall plot.

Asuka Langley Soryu: Another Eva pilot, Asuka is a complex character whose development is followed throughout the film.

World

Evangelion: The Eva are giant bio-robotic mechas designed to fight the Angels, mysterious entities that threaten humanity. Eva is also the focus of the film's plot and characters.

Angels: Angels are extraterrestrial creatures who attack Earth. Each of them has unique abilities and represents an existential threat.

NERV: A military and scientific organization that fights the Angels using Eva. She is the center of the action in the film.

Instrumentality: The process of merging humanity into a state of collective consciousness, sought by several of the film's characters.
